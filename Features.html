Core Features of SHYMU

  • RTI (Random Thoughts & Ideas): Capture and organize thoughts instantly.
  • Body Response Tracker: Log food, supplements, and track symptoms.
  • AutoForm™: Smart hydration system that makes custom water blends.
  • Race Mode: Track racing session details, share select data, and manage tasks.
  • Hydrate & Go™: Track hydration, log drinks, get clean water recipes.
  • Media Engine: AI logging for moments via car cameras (e.g., Tesla).
  • Send to SHYMU™: Forward data from other AIs like ChatGPT or Alexa into SHYMU.
